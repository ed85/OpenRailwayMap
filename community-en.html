OpenRailwayMap

Community & News

IRC channel

On the IRC channel you can receive support for all your questions related to railway mapping or using railroad data. Discussions about other topics concerning railways and OpenStreetMap are also welcome. A prior registration is not necessary. It is also possible to use a web client.

Wiki

Our OpenStreetMap wiki pages are the most important collection of information about the project. These pages provide a documentation of the data scheme, mapping guidelines and much more.

Events

The project members do not just work together online, but also meet in reallife. Frequently the developers of the project participate in hacking events or organise mapping parties. Upcoming events are announced on Twitter and our mailing list.

Mailing list

The most important communication channel is our mailing list. Official news by the developers and maintainers are posted there. The archive can be read without subscription. To send posts and receive other posts automatically it is necessary to subscribe to the mailing list.

Twitter

Short news, the latest features and pending events are published on our twitter account.

Blog

In the blog you can find detailled articles about new features, past events and background information. It is the best way to follow the project without subscribing the mailing list. The blog can also be subscribed using the RSS feed.
